## ⚙️ Core Mechanics You Must Understand

### 1. Lethality > Attack
The damage formula uses **both Attack and Lethality as multipliers**, but Lethality is typically much lower and harder to boost — meaning it provides a **disproportionately bigger return** when increased.

> **Priority:** Always focus on boosting **Lethality** first. A small Lethality increase gives more damage than the same increase in Attack.

### 2. Archers Are Best
- Archers have the **highest base Lethality** of all troop types
- The Bear is classified as an **Infantry unit** → Archers receive a **+10% Ranged Strike bonus** against Infantry
- The Bear never kills your troops, so Archer survivability is irrelevant

**Recommended Formation:**

| Troop Type | Ratio |
|---|---|
| **Infantry** | 10% |
| **Cavalry** | 10% |
| **Archers** | 80% |

> 💡 The damage formula uses the **square root of troop count**, so stacking 100% Archers gives diminishing returns. Keeping some Cavalry adds meaningful damage without wasting troop slots. Don't go below 1–5% Infantry entirely as some hero attack bonuses require infantry to activate.

### 3. The Rally Cap — Only 4 Joiner Skills Count
Each rally can receive buffs from a maximum of **4 joiner hero skills**. The 5th, 6th, etc. joiners contribute troops but **no additional hero skill bonuses**.

> This is why **quality beats quantity** for joiner slots.

### 4. Only the First Hero Slot's Expedition Skill Counts for Joiners
When you join a rally, only the **first expedition skill of the hero in slot 1** activates. Hero skills in slots 2 and 3 do not contribute as joiner bonuses.

### 5. Rally Leader Stats Apply to Everyone
The leader's stats — combat research, Governor Gear, hero equipment, pet bonuses, city buffs, consumables — all apply to **every troop in the rally**. This is why only your **strongest players** should host rallies.

## 🏰 Pitfall Upgrade — Always Max This First

Upgrading the Pitfall gives a flat **+Attack bonus to all troops** in every rally.

| Pitfall Level | Hunting Arrows Required | Attack Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 200 | +5% |
| 2 | 300 | +10% |
| 3 | 400 | +15% |
| 4 | 500 | +20% |
| **5 (Max)** | **500** | **+25%** |

**Total to max:** 1,900 Hunting Arrows

- Collect Hunting Arrows from **Intel Missions** (up to 24 arrows/day per player)
- If 40 members complete daily Intel Missions, the Alliance can max the Pitfall every 2 days
- After max level, **extra donations are lost** — don't over-donate

> 📌 Remind your whole Alliance to complete Intel Missions daily. A Level 5 Pitfall gives a free +25% Attack to every single rally.

## 🦸 Hero Setup — Gen 1

### 🏳️ Rally Leader / Host Heroes (Gen 1)

The Rally Leader's heroes buff **everyone** in the rally. Use your highest-stat heroes.

| Player Type | Best Combo | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Spender (Best)** | Amadeus, Jabel, Saul | Top raw stats for Gen 1 |
| **Spender (Alt)** | Helga, Jabel, Saul | Use if Helga is stronger than Amadeus |
| **F2P** | Howard, Jabel, Quinn | Best available without SSR heroes |

> ⚠️ **The Amadeus Dilemma (Gen 1):**
> - Amadeus is the best **Rally Leader** for raw stats in Gen 1
> - BUT his first Expedition Skill is also excellent as a **joiner** (Lethality boost)
> - If your **Helga is strong enough** (good stars + widget), let her host the rally and send Amadeus to **join rallies** instead — you'll get more total damage across the event
> - Each player should evaluate their own hero strengths to decide

### 📋 Joiner Decision Flowchart

```
Does the rally already have 4 DamageUp heroes? (Chenko/Amadeus/Yeonwoo/Amane)
         │
        NO → Send your best DamageUp hero in slot 1
         │
        YES → Send a Blue (common) hero in slot 1
              (ensures max troop capacity via Drill Camp level)

              that could override an existing Level 5 DamageUp skill
```

> 💡 **How to check:** Tap the yellow flag icon on the rally page and scroll down. Confirmed Level 5 skills are locked in and cannot be replaced unless that player is kicked.

## ⚔️ Event Strategy

### Before the Event
- [ ] Max the **Pitfall** with Hunting Arrows
- [ ] Recall all gathering troops
- [ ] Activate city buffs: **Troop Lethality** and **Troop Attack** before launching rallies
- [ ] Teleport strong Rally Leader cities **close to the Pitfall** to reduce march time
- [ ] Set up **Formation Presets** in your profile for quick deployment

### During the Event

**Who Should Host Rallies?**
- Only the **strongest players** (highest total combat stats) should launch rallies
- Do **NOT** start a rally if your city is **more than 5 minutes** from the Pitfall
- Rally march time should be **under 40 seconds** to maximize consecutive hits within 30 minutes

**Managing Rallies:**
- Avoid opening too many rallies at once — it splits joiners and dilutes joiner hero skill bonuses
- **Actively monitor** who joins your rally — kick players who join with defensive heroes (Howard, Gordon, Fahd)
- It is better to have **fewer players with correct setups** than a full rally with wasted joiner slots

**The Final 5–7 Minutes:**
- Every member should **launch their own rally** in the last 5–7 minutes
- This creates more slots for players whose troops are returning from earlier marches
- Maximum damage push for the final stretch

### After Each Wave
- Recall march immediately after the rally resolves
- Send troops out again as fast as possible — **march time is the limiting factor**
- No personal cooldown between hits — only your march speed matters

## 💡 Advanced Tips

### Territory Banners — Do NOT Apply
Territory bonuses only apply when **defending your city**. In Bear Hunt you are the attacker, so all banner bonuses are inactive.

### Shields — Not Needed
The Bear does not attack your city. There is no need to use a Shield.

### Secondary Marches
After joining the main rally with your best setup, send additional marches to other rallies. Even without top heroes, extra troops contribute to total damage.

### City Buffs — Activate Before Launching
Consumable Troop Lethality and Troop Attack buffs apply to **every troop in your rally** as the leader. Activate them before starting your rally for maximum effect.

### Reward Tiers — Play Smart
- Pay attention to damage **thresholds** for reward tiers
- If you are close to the next tier, prioritize joining rallies with strong leaders
- If you are far from the next tier, consider helping alliance members reach their milestone instead
