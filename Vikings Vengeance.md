# ⚔️ Vikings Vengeance — Quick Alliance Guide

## Main Strategy
- Empty your city as much as possible.
- Send your troops as **reinforcements** to alliance members.
- Prioritize reinforcing **online players**.
- **Infantry + Cavalry** are the most important troop types.
- Keep only a **small defensive setup** at home.
- Before **Wave 10** and **Wave 20**, send a strong march to the **HQ**.
- **Do not heal troops during the event**.

## Why this works
- Personal points come from killing Vikings.
- If your own city has too many troops, they steal kills from allied reinforcements.
- Reinforcing others gives you extra value without reducing their score.

## Best Practice
- Leave only your **3 best defensive heroes** at home.
- Send the rest of your troops out to reinforce.
- If you cannot send everything, send **Infantry and Cavalry first**.

## Important Waves
- **Wave 10** = HQ
- **Wave 20** = HQ

After **Wave 9** and **Wave 19**:
- recall one strong march,
- send it to the **HQ**,
- then send it back to reinforce allies after the HQ wave.

## Recommended Reinforcement Heroes
Use strong attack / joiner-style heroes when reinforcing, such as:
- Chenko
- Amadeus
- Yeonwoo
- Amane

Defensive-only heroes are usually less effective for reinforcement scoring.

## Very Short Version
**Empty city -> reinforce allies -> prioritize online members -> Infantry/Cavalry first -> prepare for HQ on 10 and 20 -> no healing during event.**
