## 🧮 How Points Work

This is the most misunderstood mechanic. Here's how it actually works:

### Defense Points
- Awarded to the **city owner** for successfully defending a wave
- Amount is **fixed** — does not matter who gets the kills
- Even with 0 troops at home, you still receive full Defense Points as long as your city isn't burned

### Kill / Reinforcement Points
- Awarded to the **player who reinforces** another city
- Based on the number of kills their troops get inside the target city
- Reinforcing **does NOT steal** points from the city owner
- It creates **extra bonus points** for the reinforcer — both sides benefit

### ✅ Key Takeaway
> The city owner gets full Defense Points. The reinforcer gets Kill Points. **Both players win.** This is why the "empty city" strategy is optimal for your entire Alliance.

## 🏠 Preparation Checklist

### ✅ Do This Before the Event
- Place your **3 strongest heroes** in the **Guard Station** at home
- Send all **Infantry & Cavalry** out to reinforce allies
- Share your coordinates in Alliance chat for easy reinforcement
- Coordinate HQ captains for Wave 10 and Wave 20
- Stay **online** for the full ~40 minutes

### ❌ Never Do This
- Do **not** heal troops during the event
- Do **not** activate a Shield (it doesn't work in this event)
- Do **not** extinguish fires after losing once (wasteful — see details below)
- Do **not** have troops gathering or reinforcing others right before the event starts if you plan to go offline

## ⚔️ Troop Strategy

### Priority Order for Sending Troops Out
1. **Infantry** — Attacks first, highest kill count against low-health Vikings
2. **Cavalry** — Strong secondary damage, especially in higher waves
3. **Archers** — Leave at home if marches are full; they get few kills and won't significantly steal points from reinforcers

### Formation
There is **no specific recommended ratio** for normal reinforcement waves. Simply:
- Use the **Balance button** to fill marches proportionally
- Prioritize filling Infantry and Cavalry slots first
- Any troops left at home will fight Vikings and **reduce kill credit** for players reinforcing you — so empty your city as much as possible

## 🌊 Wave-by-Wave Action Guide

### Normal Waves (1–6, 8–9, 11–13, 15–16, 18–19)
- Send all marches (up to 6 queues) out to reinforce allies
- Prioritize **online** players who have **emptied their cities**
- Leave only Archers + Guard Station heroes at home

### Elite Waves — Online Only (7, 14, 17)
- Before the wave: confirm your target is **currently online**
- Vikings move slower in these waves — you have time to switch targets
- If your target is offline, immediately redirect your marches to an online player

### HQ Waves (10 and 20)

```
Wave 9 fully ends
       ↓
Recall your 1 strongest march with best available heroes
       ↓
Send that march to reinforce the Alliance HQ
       ↓
Wait for Wave 10 to fully resolve at HQ
       ↓
Recall march from HQ → return to reinforcing a city
       ↓
Repeat the same process before Wave 20
```

**HQ Rules:**
- **Plains HQ** is preferred over other HQ types
- The **HQ Captain** must be the player with the highest rally capacity in the Alliance
- Send only **Infantry and Cavalry** to HQ — no Archers
- Do **not recall early** — if you leave before the wave resolves, you lose all points for that wave

## 🔥 Critical Rules — Do Not Ignore

### 1. Never Heal During the Event
Troops healed in the Hospital return immediately to your city and start stealing kills from players reinforcing you — directly reducing their points.

**Exception:** You may heal between waves if absolutely necessary, but only if you **immediately send those troops back out** before the next wave begins.

### 2. Do Not Extinguish Fires After Losing Once
- Your city is only fully eliminated after being burned **twice**
- After the 1st burn, you can still host reinforcements and earn points
- Extinguishing costs **Diamonds** but provides **no strategic benefit**
- Only put out fires after the event ends or after your 2nd defeat

### 3. If You Cannot Be Online
If you cannot attend the event:
- Make sure your troops are **inside your city** at event start time
- Do not leave them gathering or reinforcing others
- Your city will be attacked normally on waves 1–6, 8–9, etc.
- You will simply be skipped on Elite Waves 7, 14, and 17
- Best option: reinforce alliance members before you log off

---

## 🏰 What Happens When Your City is Burned Twice
- Vikings **stop attacking** your city
- You are **not eliminated** from earning points
- Immediately send all 6 marches out to reinforce other players
- You can keep earning Kill Points for the remainder of the event
